ABOUT US

Real People. Real Problems. Real Solutions!

WorkMate is a service-driven platform designed to bridge the gap between individuals in need of daily help and skilled local workers who often lack digital visibility. The idea originated from a personal experience — after moving from a college hostel to a flat, I struggled to find a reliable maid. This challenge revealed a widespread problem: most essential service workers are not accessible online, and many people relocating to new areas face the same issue. To address this, WorkMate was created as a simple, accessible, and secure solution that directly connects customers with nearby workers who offer daily services like cleaning, cooking, plumbing, and more.
